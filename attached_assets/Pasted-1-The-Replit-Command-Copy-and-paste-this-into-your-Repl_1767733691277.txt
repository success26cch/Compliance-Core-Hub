2. The "Why" (The Pitch for Your Retainer)
When you speak to a company about the retainer, use these 3 Big Reasons. They focus on the CEO's biggest fears: Fines, Lawsuits, and Audits.

Reason 1: AI is for Guidance, Humans are for Liability
The Pitch: "The app is great for knowing the difference between a 5-panel and 10-panel drug screen. But if an employee tests positive and you’re about to fire them, you need a human consultant to verify your 'Fit-for-Duty' policy. We take the liability off your shoulders."

Reason 2: Avoid the 'Red Flag' Audit
The Pitch: "Most OSHA fines happen because of simple paperwork errors on the 300 Log. By having us on retainer, we review your logs monthly. We find the 'red flags' before the government does. We aren't just an expense; we are an insurance policy against $15,000+ fines."

Reason 3: The "Peace of Mind" Factor
The Pitch: "Accidents don't happen during business hours. For a small monthly retainer, you have a professional safety officer's cell phone number. When a crisis happens at 2 AM on a job site, you aren't Googling—you're calling us."
