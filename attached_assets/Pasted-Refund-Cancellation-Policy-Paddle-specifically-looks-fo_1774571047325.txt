Refunds & Subscription Cancellations

Cancellation: You may cancel your Core Compliance Hub (CCH) subscription at any time directly through your account dashboard or by contacting support. Your access will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Refunds: Since CCH provides immediate access to digital training curriculum and compliance tools, we generally do not offer refunds once a billing cycle has processed. However, if you experience technical issues that prevent you from using the service, please contact us within 14 days of the charge for a full review.

Invoiced Payments: For companies paying via monthly invoice, payments are due within 30 days. Late payments may result in a temporary suspension of platform access until the balance is cleared.
